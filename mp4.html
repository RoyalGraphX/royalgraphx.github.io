August 22, 2017

?多?多

After a conversation between me and Gustavo, we finally decided on a release date. We began to work on preparing for their return. It allowed me to improve on a long lost dream of mine to work in video production; I swore up and down I knew what i was doing, It was a lie, but I was always committed to getting what needed to be done, done. Just a day after the initial release of '?多?多' , I had already began getting comfortable in After Effects, not realising it was a compositing program and not for video production, I quickly wanted to switch to Premier Pro, strangley enough I never was able to do it. October 22, 2017

A Day with BWF

This video was purely recorded at the park, They had the items necessary to make a video, so they took that idea and ran with it. When I got my hands on the clips for this date's shoot, I immedietely knew what I wanted the video to look like, A smooth 60fps was used on these clips and when rendered, left the intro to the video one of my favorites of all time! I mean just admire that camera pan out. Its gorgeous.
