About Me

I'm a 17 year old Photographer, and visual effects editor, although I've only recently been posting photos, my main goal is actually still in Cinematography, the art of making motion pictures. Being a Chicagoan, I've never had a lack of rooftops and gorgeous nighttime scenes, but rather a lack of other collaborators who wish to go out and capture some amazing moments. All I need is a text.

Collaboration is key!

Tell me what you want done, and I can try my best to get it done. If you're wanting a music video done, and need props, or specific scenes, let me know! Talking is crucial to getting the perfect end result you want, and mine.

Good Pricing

I pride myself in giving others the benefit of the doubt, If I do work for you and it's either discounted or not up to what I believe the work is worth, then I trust you will do your part to get us both seen, It's not always about the money.
